Who we are?

Tejas Corp Pvt. Ltd stands today as a proud extension of the legacy built by the KGS Group - a journey that began humbly over 45 years ago with a single grocery shop, Kedar General Store. What started as a small, community-centered business in Mahendranagar has grown into a thriving group of companies that touch the lives of thousands across Nepal.

The journey of KGS Group is a story of vision, resilience, and relentless commitment to growth. Rooted in honesty and built with the trust of the people, the business expanded step by step - never forgetting its origins, and always driven by the desire to serve. From its beginnings in retail, KGS Group ventured into trading, becoming an authorized distributor for iconic brands such as Tuborg, Khukuri Rum, Gurkhas & Guns, and Pepsi. This marked the first step toward becoming a regional powerhouse in distribution.

Over the years, the group has grown into multiple specialized ventures:
  • K.G.S Tradelink Pvt. Ltd. (Mahendranagar): Authorized dealer of TVS three-wheelers
  • KGS Trading Concern (Mahendranagar): Authorized dealer of Yamaha two-wheelers
  • GARS Trading Concern (Dhangadhi): Authorized dealer of Nissan four-wheelers
  • LS Water and Beverages (Dhangadhi) : Producers of energy and carbonated drinks
  • KGS Home Decor: Suppliers of home appliances for modern living
  • Kundaleshwor Films Pvt. Ltd.: Creators of powerful stories through documentary films

Now, continuing this legacy of innovation and customer service, Tejas Corp Pvt. Ltd has been established as a dedicated provider of inverter batteries, tubular batteries, automotive batteries, and related appliances - serving both homes and industries with reliable energy solutions.

At the heart of everything we do is a simple belief: growth must serve people. From our first grocery customer to the latest car buyer, our story has always been about delivering trust, quality, and connection.

We are not just a group of businesses - we are a generational dream, carried forward with honesty, and built to last.

Welcome to Tejas Corp Pvt. Ltd. Welcome to the future of power, built on the foundation of your trust.
